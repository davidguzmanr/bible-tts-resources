Portions of the Holy Bible in the Toma language of Guinea

Des portions de la Sainte Bible dans la langue de la Guinée Toma

Tɔɔzeizu mɔunpa GALA ge geeɠɔlɔgi ta zooi ɓɛtɛni.

—Genesis 1:1

Davide ná-guyei. Nà ga baalagi, Ɠɔoɠɔ GALAGI ɠa é ga màkɛ nui, gè la ʋalaga ani nɔpɛ ma.

—Psalm 23:1

Kɛlɛ, à GALA ná-masadai ta ná-telebodai ɠaiziɛ mɔunpa, naa ʋoluma naa pɛ ka la maazu, é ve wo ya.

—Matthew 6:33

«Mazɔlɔɔ GALA ge eteai nɛa ʋɛ, eyɛsu é ná-Doun Zunu ɠila kpegi vea, naa ɠa a kɛ nu nɔpɛ a la da, naa undaa mina ʋili, kɛlɛ é ɠɔoɠɔ zɛnvui zɔlɔɔ.

—John 3:16

De kwɛni de ɠɛ, GALA ka botii ɠɛɛzu fai pɛ su, é ɠɛ ga faa ʋagɔ naati bɛ, niiti ti nɛa ʋɛ, ti loligai eɠɛʋelei ná-devei la.

—Romans 8:28
